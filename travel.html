Buses will arrive in front of the George Sherman Union. Walk to the building where the buses park and go up the main staircase.

  Plane

Arrival

Take the Silver Line to South Station.

At South Station, take the Red Line toward Alewife and get off Park Street.

At Park Street, take the Green Line - B Branch toward Boston College and get off Boston University Central.

Departure

Walk to Boston University Central and take the Green Line to Park Street.

At Park Street, take the Red Line to South Station.

At South Station, take the Silver Line to Logan International Airport.
