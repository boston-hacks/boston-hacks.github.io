FAQ
What's the wifi?
Wifi: BU Events Guest
Password: H4ckA!!D4y
Where is the hacking area?
George Sherman Union, 2nd Floor in Metcalf Hall. Check our map page.
Are there any rules?
We want to ensure a positive experience for every participant. We encourage you to read our Code of Conduct.
What should I bring?
You should bring your laptop, phone, chargers, and sleeping bag. All food and other accommodations will be taken care of.
Can we form teams?
Of course you can! We encourage people to work in teams of up to 4 people. You can work alone, but it won't be the same.
What do I do if I don't have a team?
There will be a team-forming event after the opening ceremony to help singletons form teams. It will be located in the BackCourt on the 1st floor of the GSU at 1:45 PM on Saturday. Check our schedule page
Does it cost anything?
BostonHacks is 100% free. You won't have to spend a dime! Everything will be absolutely free.
How am I going to eat and sleep?
Free meals will be provided for the hackathon, along with lots (lots) of snacks throughout!
How/When do I need to submit my project?
All projects will need to be submitted on Devpost by 11:30am on Sunday although you can continue to work on your demonstration after you submit. Instructions for submitting on Devpost will be sent out during the hackathon.
Will there be travel reimbursements?
We're working on providing travel reimbursements, and we will provide the form later.
