  Parking

Park at 808 Commonwealth Avenue's parking lot. You will have to pay $12 for the day or $24 overnight, and this will be reimbursed by check in 1-2 weeks. Keep the parking receipt.

  Bus

Arrival

Buses will arrive in front of the George Sherman Union. Walk to the building and go up the main staircase.

Departure

Buses will depart at 4:00 PM to Toronto, Waterloo, and Rutgers in front of the George Sherman Union.

  Plane

Arrival

Take the Silver Line to South Station.

At South Station, take the Red Line toward Alewife and get off Park Street.

At Park Street, take the Green Line - B Branch toward Boston College and get off Boston University Central.

Departure

Walk to Boston University Central and take the Green Line to Park Street.

At Park Street, take the Red Line to South Station.

At South Station, take the Silver Line to Logan International Airport.
